Description

A colorful, tasty dish. Served with garlic bread, this vegetarian lasagne makes an excellent meal for guests.

Ingredients

  • dough
  • meat
  • courgette

Steps

  1. Turn on the oven
  2. Slice the meat
  3. Slice the courgette
  4. Put into bowl
  5. Put into oven
  6. Enjoy
