Kapsalon

Description

A dish which originated in Rotterdam after a hairdresser came by each day ordering this specific dish.

"Kapsalon" translates to "Hairdresser"

Ingredients

  • Fries
  • Doner
  • Cheese
  • lettuce
  • Garlic sauce
  • Spicy sauce

Steps

  1. Pre heat the oven at 200 degrees celcius
  2. Fry the fries
  3. Cook the meat
  4. Put the fries and meat in a bowl
  5. Put cheese on it and put it in the oven until the cheese melts
  6. Put the letuce and sauces on the dish as desired
  7. Enjoy
